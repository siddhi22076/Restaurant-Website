About Aroma

Established in the heart of Solapur, Aroma Restaurant is known for its premium ambiance, exquisite cuisines, and exceptional service. We specialize in Indian, Continental, and fusion delicacies, crafted using fresh ingredients and rich spices.

Whether it's a family dinner, romantic evening, or corporate gathering, Aroma offers an unforgettable fine dining experience in a luxurious and soothing dark-themed environment.
